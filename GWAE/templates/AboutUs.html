About GWAE

Our Mission:


Our mission is to encourage as many people as we can to take action about global warming and make our society more eco-friendly.

Our Vision:


We envision a world where an eco-friendly lifestyle is very common and normal, where people care about the state of the environment and global warming is no longer a problem.
